Griffin's Personal Movie List

My Top Three Favorite Movies

Rurouni Kenshin: The Beginning

I thoroughly enjoyed Rurouni Kenshin for the action and for it being a live action anime.

Kill Boksoon

Kill Boksoon has a very enticing story about a mother who is actually apart of a secret society and an assassin.

Believer

Believer is a movie about a burnt out detective in pursuit of a drug kingpin who hides his identity. Luckily for the detective he gets a break when a survivor from a factory explosion decides to help.
